Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe, also known as XO or X and O, is a pencil-and-paper game played by two players. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, and players take turns placing their symbols (usually X or O) into the grid. The goal is to get three of the same symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How to Play

Playing tic-tac-toe is quite simple. Players take turns placing their symbols (usually X or O) into an empty cell on the 3x3 grid. The player who first manages to get three of the same symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, is the winner. If all cells are filled without a winner, the game is considered a draw.
